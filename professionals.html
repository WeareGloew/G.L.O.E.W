Working with G.L.O.E.W

G.L.O.E.W is a trauma-informed, early-intervention programme supporting young women aged 18–25 who have experienced, or are at risk of:

  • Domestic abuse (DA)
  • Sexual violence (SV)
  • Emotional harm
  • Unhealthy relationships
  • Housing instability
  • Exploitation

Rooted in psychological safety, sisterhood, and empowerment, the programme helps participants rebuild confidence, strengthen identity, develop healthy boundaries, and access opportunities in education, employment and wellbeing.

Our Model

Trauma-informed, holistic, evidence-linked

12-week Cohort Programme

Structured, progressive curriculum blending therapeutic circles, practical workshops, and experiential learning.

Strength-based Approach

We centre autonomy, emotional safety, and culturally affirming practice.

Measurable Outcomes

Participants show improvements in self-esteem, emotional regulation, healthy relationship patterns and future planning.
