About G.L.O.E.W

G.L.O.E.W is a trauma-informed sisterhood space for young women who are ready to heal, grow, and step into the soft, stable lives they deserve.

Our heart

Why G.L.O.E.W exists

G.L.O.E.W was created from lived experience — as a response to the gaps so many young women fall through when it comes to healing, safety, confidence, and real-life opportunities.

For the girl who…

Has survived more than she’s ever said out loud.

We hold space for young women who may have experienced domestic abuse, sexual violence, emotional harm, unstable relationships, or childhood trauma — and who are ready to move from surviving to slowly, gently thriving.

We believe…

You shouldn’t have to heal alone.

Healing is hard, especially in isolation. G.L.O.E.W is built as a sisterhood: a community where women can breathe, unpack, laugh, cry, and rebuild together — supported by trauma-informed practice, lived experience, and practical tools.

Values

Our core values

Safety

Emotional + physical safety first.

We prioritise consent, confidentiality, boundaries and emotional check-ins. No one is pushed to share more than they want to.

Dignity

You are more than what happened to you.

We see every woman as whole — not broken. We focus on strengths, resilience, gifts and the future, not just on the pain.

Sisterhood

No one left behind.

G.L.O.E.W builds real, honest connections. Women celebrate each other, challenge harmful patterns, and hold each other accountable with love.

Growth

Small steps count.

We celebrate every win: a boundary, a red flag noticed, a CV updated, a poem performed. Growth is emotional and practical.

For Professionals & Funders

G.L.O.E.W supports young women aged 18–25 who have experienced, or are at risk of, domestic abuse, sexual trauma, emotional neglect, or community-based trauma.

We partner with statutory services, youth organisations, local authorities, VAWG services, commissioners and charities committed to early intervention, safeguarding and empowerment.

Our programme is built using:

  • Trauma-informed practice
  • Safeguarding-first frameworks
  • Reflective therapeutic tools
  • Confidence-building workshops
  • Holistic emotional + practical learning
  • Evidence-based empowerment models

For partnership conversations, commissioning queries or programme information packs, reach out via our contact page.
