The G.L.O.E.W 12-week journey

A carefully designed programme blending healing, confidence, relationships, safety, money skills and soft-life moments — so growth isn’t just talked about, it’s lived.

Overview

Three phases of the cohort

Phase 1

Weeks 1–4: Safety, stories & self-awareness.

Welcome day, icebreakers, personality tools, story sharing and reflective writing on life plans, trauma responses and identity.

Phase 2

Weeks 5–8: Confidence, body & voice.

Spoken word training, “Lay it Bare” circles, movement workshops (including pole art for confidence), self-defence and safety awareness.

Phase 3

Weeks 9–12: Future, freedom & soft life.

Money mindset, financial literacy, opportunity pathways, reflection, and a soft-life celebration to honour growth and next steps.

Week by week

Sample cohort outline

Week 1

Welcome, story mapping & confidence check-in.

Icebreakers, personality tools, writing exercises on trauma responses and life goals, group agreements and pre-programme evaluation.

Week 2

Money, independence & imposter syndrome.

Financial literacy, practical budgeting skills, and mindset work around worthiness in education, work and money.

Week 3

Education, work & opportunity.

CV support, training pathways, course referrals and discussions about future goals and next steps.

Week 4

Skills & creativity.

Practical tasters (lash/beauty workshops, book club start), early confidence building and hobby exploration.

Weeks 5–6

“Lay It Bare” & body confidence.

Therapeutic circles, letters to people who have caused harm, healthy relationship mapping and movement-based confidence sessions.

Weeks 7–8

Safety & voice.

Self-defence training, safety apps/tools, and spoken-word development.

Weeks 9–11

Integration & reflection.

Confidence follow-ups, performing spoken pieces in circle, group feedback, structured reflection and affirmations.

Week 12

Soft-life celebration.

Group treat (meal or experience), final reflections, feedback forms, signposting and onward support planning.
