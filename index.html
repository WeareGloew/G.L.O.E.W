About G.L.O.E.W

What is G.L.O.E.W?

Trauma-informed, but not trauma-defined.

A space where young women can breathe, heal, connect and grow — supported by sisterhood, structure and care.

Healing + skills + sisterhood.

We blend therapeutic circles, confidence-building, creative expression, practical life skills, and joyful experiences.
