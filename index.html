About G.L.O.E.W

G.L.O.E.W is a trauma-informed sisterhood space designed for young women ready to heal, grow and step into the soft, stable lives they deserve.

Our heart

Why G.L.O.E.W exists

Born from lived experience — G.L.O.E.W responds to the gaps young women fall through when seeking healing, confidence, safety and opportunity.

For the girl who…

Has survived more than she’s ever said out loud.

We support young women who may have experienced domestic abuse, sexual violence, emotional harm or childhood trauma — gently walking with them from surviving to thriving.

We believe…

You shouldn’t have to heal alone.

G.L.O.E.W is a sisterhood: a place to breathe, unpack, laugh, cry and rebuild. Created using trauma-informed practice and lived experience.

Values

Our core values

Safety

Emotional & physical safety first.

We prioritise consent, boundaries, confidentiality and emotional check-ins.

Dignity

You are more than what happened to you.

We focus on resilience, strengths, gifts and the future — not the pain alone.

Sisterhood

No one left behind.

Women celebrate one another, challenge harmful patterns and support with love.

Growth

Small steps matter.

Every boundary, every realisation, every moment of confidence is recognised.

For professionals

Professionals & Funders

We collaborate with social workers, housing teams, youth workers, safeguarding leads, commissioners and organisations supporting young women.

  • Trauma-informed practice
  • Safeguarding-first approach
  • Reflective therapeutic exercises
  • Holistic emotional & practical learning
  • Healthy relationship education
