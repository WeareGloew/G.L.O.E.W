About G.L.O.E.W

What is G.L.O.E.W?

Our heart

Trauma-informed, not trauma-defined.

A safe, judgement-free space for young women who have experienced or are at risk of emotional harm, abuse, or unhealthy relationships — and are ready to reclaim their identity, autonomy and joy.

Our approach

Healing + skills + sisterhood.

G.L.O.E.W blends therapeutic circles, creative expression, emotional development, practical life skills and soft-life experiences to build confidence from the inside out.

The journey

Inside the 12-week G.L.O.E.W cohort

Weeks 1–4

Safety, stories & self-awareness.

Welcome circles, icebreakers, personality tools, and gentle introduction to identity, boundaries and understanding trauma responses.

Weeks 5–8

Confidence, body & voice.

Spoken word workshops, movement + embodied healing sessions, self-defence training, and redefining what healthy love looks like.

Weeks 9–12

Future, freedom & soft life.

Money + independence, education and work opportunities, celebration of growth, and closing rituals honouring each woman’s story.

Who G.L.O.E.W is for

G.L.O.E.W is for young women aged 18–25 who want to understand themselves more deeply, build safer relationships, strengthen their identity, and heal with support — not in isolation.
